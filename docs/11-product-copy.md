## 1. Core Messaging

### Product Name
**Kairo**

### Tagline
> *"Your plan adapts. You stay consistent."*

### One-Liner
> Set your real-life constraints — time, equipment, stress. Get a daily workout + protein plan. Log in 30 seconds. Tomorrow auto-adjusts.

### Elevator Pitch
> Kairo generates a daily workout + nutrition plan from your real constraints — and adapts tomorrow when today doesn't go as planned. Unlike rigid 12-week programs that break when you miss a day, Kairo adapts your plan daily — because consistency beats perfection.

## 5. Feature Descriptions

| Feature | Headline | Description |
|---------|----------|-------------|
| Constraints Engine | Your rules, your plan | Input time, equipment, context — get a plan that fits |
| Daily Plan | Options, not orders | 2–3 workout alternatives + protein targets daily |

| Auto-Adapt | Miss a day? Tomorrow's smarter | Plan adjusts based on what actually happened |

## 6. Tone Guidelines

- **Voice:** Confident but not pushy. Empathetic. Direct.
- **Avoid:** Guilt, shame, "no excuses" language, fitness bro culture
- **Embrace:** Flexibility, realism, consistency over perfection
- **Numbers:** Use concrete values (grams, minutes, percentages) — never vague

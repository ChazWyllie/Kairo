# Kairo — Solution Slide Content

> **For:** Pitch deck / investor presentation
> **Version:** 1.0
> **Date:** 2026-02-15

---

## Headline
**Kairo**

## Tagline
> *"Your plan adapts. You stay consistent."*

## One-liner Value Prop
> Kairo generates a daily workout + nutrition plan from your real constraints — and adapts tomorrow when today doesn't go as planned.

---

## 3-Step User Flow

| Step | Action | Detail |
|------|--------|--------|
| **1** | **Set your constraints** | Time, equipment, travel, stress level — takes 10 seconds |
| **2** | **Get today's plan** | 2–3 workout options + protein target (160g) + meal suggestions |
| **3** | **Log in 30 seconds** | Check off workout, meals, water, steps — tomorrow adapts automatically |

### Element Placement Guide
- **Top third:** Product name (large, bold) + tagline (italic, smaller) + one-liner
- **Center:** 3 phone mockups side-by-side (equal width, slight shadow)
- **Below mockups:** 3-step flow as horizontal arrow sequence
- **Bottom:** Competitor contrast quote (italic, muted color)
- **Background:** Dark (#0a0a0f) or gradient, white text
- **Accent color:** Electric blue (#4f8cff) for buttons and highlights

12) The scripts you should use

You asked what script to use. Here are the exact scripts/templates.

Script 1: new lead auto-response

Subject:
Application Received

Body:
“Hey [Name], I got your application and appreciate you reaching out. I’ll review everything and get back to you soon with the next steps. If we move forward, I’ll make sure you know exactly what to expect from coaching.”

Script 2: approved lead

“Hey [Name], after reviewing your application, I think you’d be a strong fit for coaching. Based on your goals and what support you need, I’d recommend the [Tier Name] plan. Once payment is complete, I’ll send over your onboarding form and next steps so we can get started properly.”

Script 3: onboarding sent

“Welcome aboard. Your next step is to complete the onboarding form as thoroughly as possible. The more accurate and detailed you are, the better I can tailor your plan. Once I review it, I’ll build your starting setup and send over your training and nutrition guidance.”

Script 4: weekly check-in reminder

“Hey [Name], just a reminder to submit your weekly check-in today so I can review your progress and make any needed adjustments. The more consistent your check-ins are, the better I can coach you.”

Script 5: weekly review response

Use this exact structure every time.

Template

Win:
“Strong job getting your sessions in this week and keeping protein consistent.”

Data:
“Your average weight was [X], steps were [X], and training adherence was [X].”

Decision:
“We’re keeping calories the same and pushing performance this week.”
or
“We’re lowering calories by [X] and increasing daily steps by [X].”

Focus:
“Your main focus this week is [3 priorities max].”

Close:
“You’re moving in the right direction. Stay patient and execute.”

Script 6: form review delivery

“Just sent over your form review. Main things to focus on: [point 1], [point 2], and [point 3]. Apply these next session and send me another clip when ready.”

Script 7: client slipping / re-engagement

“Hey [Name], I wanted to check in because I noticed things have been slipping a bit. No judgment at all — I just want to help you get back on track. Let’s simplify things and focus on the few actions that matter most this week.”

Script 8: monthly review

“This month, your biggest improvements were [X]. Your main bottleneck right now is [Y]. Going into next month, our plan is to [Z]. The goal is to keep momentum without adding unnecessary complexity.”

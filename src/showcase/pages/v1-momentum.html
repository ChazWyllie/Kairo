FAQ

Common questions

How is Kairo different from other fitness apps?

Most fitness apps give you a rigid program and shame you when you miss a day. Kairo adapts your plan daily based on your real constraints — time, equipment, energy, stress. Miss a day? We don't reset. We recalibrate.

Do I need a gym membership?

No. Kairo works with whatever you have — full gym, hotel room, park, or just your bodyweight at home. Toggle your available equipment and we'll match your workout options.

How long does daily logging take?

About 30 seconds. Check off your workout, meals, water, and steps. That's it. We designed it to be faster than opening Instagram.

What happens when I miss a workout?

Tap "I missed" — no guilt, no shame, no reset. Your plan for tomorrow automatically adjusts to keep you on track. Consistency over perfection.

Is my data private?

Absolutely. Your health data is encrypted end-to-end and never sold to third parties. You own your data, period.
